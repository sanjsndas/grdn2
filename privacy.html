Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Welcome to GreenScape Gardening Services. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or communicate with us. Please read this document carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information from and about you when you use our website or services, including:

  • Personal Identification Information: such as your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and any other details you provide when making inquiries, booking services, or subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Usage Data: information about how you use and interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent, browser type, IP address) collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Payment Information: for payments made, we use secure third-party payment processors; hence, payment details are handled securely by those providers and are not stored on our servers.
  • Communication Data: any correspondence or communication you have with us by phone, email, or through online forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our gardening services website and offerings.
  • To communicate with you regarding your inquiries, bookings, feedback, or support requests.
  • To send you service-related information, updates, promotions, newsletters, or marketing communications (only if you have opted in).
  • To improve our services, website functionality, and user experience based on your interactions.
  • To manage payments and prevent fraudulent transactions.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our website policies and terms of service.

3. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers such as payment processors, website hosting companies, IT support, or marketing tools who assist us in operating the website and conducting business. They are contractually obligated to use your information only as instructed by us and to keep it secure.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal requests, we may disclose your information to government authorities or law enforcement agencies.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and improve site functionality.

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for basic website operations like navigation and session management.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use services such as Google Analytics to collect anonymous information to understand user behavior and improve our site. These cookies do not identify you personally.
  • Marketing Cookies: These may be used to deliver relevant advertisements based on your browsing activity. We do not engage in targeted advertising ourselves but may integrate third-party tools that do.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Data Security

We take your data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. Our website uses secure SSL encryption to safeguard information transmitted online. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may ask us to update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal restrictions.
  • Right to Object or Restrict Processing: You have the right to object to or limit certain types of data processing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If you have given consent for us to process your data, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
