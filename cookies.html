Cookies Policy

Last updated: June 2024

This Cookies Policy explains how GreenScape Gardening Services ("we", "us", and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make your experience more efficient and enjoyable. Cookies can store user preferences, login information, and allow websites to analyze usage patterns.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies on our website, including:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for our website to function properly. They enable core features like page navigation, security, and access to secure areas. Without these cookies, the website cannot operate effectively.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect anonymous data about website usage such as the number of visitors, pages visited, time on site, and how visitors arrive at our site. This information helps us improve website performance and content to better serve you.
  • Functionality Cookies: Allow the website to remember choices you make (such as language preferences) to provide enhanced and personalized features.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: While GreenScape Gardening Services does not directly place advertising cookies, we may use third-party tools that do. These cookies collect information about your browsing habits to deliver relevant advertising and measure the effectiveness of campaigns.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

  • Recognize returning visitors and remember preferences to provide a seamless user experience.
  • Improve website functionality and content based on aggregated analytics data.
  • Understand how users interact with our website to optimize navigation and layout.
  • Maintain security by detecting and preventing fraudulent activities.
  • Enable third-party services that help us deliver advertisements or marketing campaigns targeted to your interests.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website may be placed by third-party services, such as Google Analytics, social media platforms, or advertising networks. These third parties may collect information about your online activities over time and across different websites to provide targeted advertising or analyze website traffic.

We carefully select our third-party partners and require them to comply with applicable privacy and data protection laws. However, we do not control these third-party cookies and recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more details on their data collection and usage practices.

5. Managing Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies by default, but you can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. How to do this varies by browser, so consult your browser's help section for instructions.

Please note that disabling or blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your user experience.

6. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. If you do not agree with our use of cookies, please adjust your browser settings or refrain from using our site.

7. Changes to This Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our cookie usage. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy.
