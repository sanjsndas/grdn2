Our Gardening Services

At GreenScape, we offer comprehensive gardening solutions tailored to your outdoor space needs. With years of experience and a passion for nature, our friendly team brings your garden to life.

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Keep your lawn healthy, green, and pristine with our expert mowing, fertilizing, aeration, and weed control services. We tailor each maintenance plan to your lawn's specific needs ensuring optimal growth and appearance.

Landscape Design & Installation

Transform your outdoor space with custom landscape designs, including planting beds, pathways, rock gardens, and water features. We combine aesthetics and functionality to give you a garden you'll love year-round.

Planting & Seasonal Color

Add vibrant color and seasonal charm to your garden with our planting services. From annual flowers to shrubs and trees, we ensure proper selection, placement, and care for long-lasting beauty.

Pruning & Shrub Care

Maintain plant health and shape with expert pruning and trimming services. Our team removes dead growth, fosters new buds, and keeps your shrubs and trees thriving while enhancing your garden’s look.

Irrigation System Installation & Repair

Ensure your plants receive adequate water with efficient and automated irrigation solutions. We install, repair, and maintain irrigation systems tailored to your garden’s specific requirements.

Garden Clean-Up & Seasonal Preparation

Prepare your garden for every season with thorough cleanup services. We remove debris, dead plants, and leaves to give your garden a fresh start and promote healthy growth.

About GreenScape Gardening

GreenScape Gardening Services is a family-owned and operated business committed to excellence in garden care and landscaping. Founded over 15 years ago, our team combines expertise, creativity, and environmental responsibility to craft outdoor spaces that truly flourish.

We believe every garden has its unique personality and potential. Our mission is to enhance that natural beauty with sustainable practices, quality plants, and dedicated care. Whether you have a tiny balcony garden or a vast backyard, we handle projects of all sizes with equal passion.

As advocates for green living, we prioritize organic solutions and water-saving techniques. Our team undergoes regular training to stay updated on the latest horticultural trends and technologies.

Choose GreenScape for friendly, reliable service with a personal touch. We treat your garden like it's our own.
